- **Fiat Payment Integration** - Native support for Stripe and traditional payment methods
- **AI Agent Marketplace** - Discover and monetize AI services
- **Credits System** - Flexible usage-based billing
- **Enhanced Developer Tools** - Improved APIs and SDKs
