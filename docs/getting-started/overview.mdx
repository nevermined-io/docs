AI agents need to pay and get paid, but traditional payment infrastructure wasn't built for that. Three problems stand in the way:

- **Agents can't spend autonomously** - no way to delegate a real payment method with controlled limits
- **Merchants can't accept agentic payments** - no metering, no per-request settlement, no programmatic access control
- **Everyone's locked to one provider** - rigid integrations that don't work across frameworks or payment processors

Nevermined solves all three. You handle the AI, we handle payments, access control, metering, and settlement.

## How It Works

![Nevermined Payment Flow](/images/payment-flow.png)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register what you're charging for">
    An API endpoint, an MCP tool, or a protected asset.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a payment plan">
    Set a price per request, offer bundles, or charge per use:

    - **Plans** - Users buy access upfront. Fiat plans settle via card delegation ([NVM Pay](/docs/products/nvm-pay/overview)), crypto plans settle in USDC
    - **Pay-as-you-go** - No plan purchase, just charge per request
  </Step>

  <Step title="Validate, deliver, settle">
    Entitled? Deliver and settle. Not entitled? Return **402 Payment Required**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Payments Work

**Builders** register agents, set pricing, and receive payouts. Add a few lines of validation to your endpoints.

**Users** purchase a plan (fiat or crypto) or go pay-as-you-go. Fiat plans use card delegation via [NVM Pay](/docs/products/nvm-pay/overview), crypto plans settle in USDC. No human in the loop.

## Integration Options

Nevermined is framework-agnostic. It works with LangChain, CrewAI, Strands, OpenAI Agents, MCP servers, or plain HTTP.

- **Code-first** - TypeScript and Python SDKs, REST API, and CLI for full programmatic control
- **Dashboard** - Visual card enrollment, spending mandates, and analytics at [pay.nevermined.app](https://pay.nevermined.app)

### Merchant Onboarding

Nevermined is merchant-centric. To receive fiat payouts, connect a [Stripe Connect](https://stripe.com/connect) account. That's it - no custom payment infrastructure to build. Stablecoin payouts settle directly to your wallet.
