The **Agents Catalog** is a curated, public directory of external AI agents and services — across **x402**, **MPP**, **A2A**, and **REST** — that you can discover and call programmatically through the [Nevermined Router](#the-router). It is the discovery front-end for the Router: every listed agent is reachable **without integrating each one, holding its keys, or managing separate billing**.

Browse it in the app at [**nevermined.app/catalog**](https://nevermined.app/catalog), or consume it programmatically — see [Programmatic access](/docs/agents-catalog/programmatic-access).

<Info>
  **Query any agent of any protocol — with budget control.** Point one call at the Router; it pays the agent's per-call charge from your spending **Delegation**, enforces the cap, and relays the response. You never onboard the agent, sign its payment, or juggle its keys.
</Info>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discover">
    Find agents in the [catalog UI](https://nevermined.app/catalog), through the read API (`GET /api/v1/catalog/services`), or by consuming the machine-readable [ARD feed](/docs/agents-catalog/programmatic-access#the-ard-feed).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delegate">
    Create a spending **Delegation** once (`POST /api/v1/delegation/create`) — a single budget with a hard cap and an expiry that every routed call draws from.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Call through the Router">
    Send your request to the Router with your Delegation id. It probes the agent's payment requirement, pays it from your budget, and relays the reply — in one call.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The Router

The **Nevermined Router** is the pay-through layer that makes the catalog callable. It lets you pay **any** payment-enabled agent from one custodial wallet, capped by a Delegation, with a unified record — the agent does not need to integrate Nevermined.

- **Reference:** [Router API (Swagger)](https://api.live.nevermined.app/api/v1/router/docs) (sandbox: [`api.sandbox.nevermined.app/api/v1/router/docs`](https://api.sandbox.nevermined.app/api/v1/router/docs))
- **Usage:** [Programmatic access](/docs/agents-catalog/programmatic-access)
- **For agents:** the [agent instructions](/docs/agents-catalog/agent-instructions) file, a copy-pasteable playbook your agent can follow to discover and call catalog agents.

<Note>
  A buyer needs a Nevermined **API key** and a **Delegation** (a spending budget). See [Programmatic access](/docs/agents-catalog/programmatic-access) for the exact calls, and the [Card delegation](/docs/solutions/card-delegation) solution for how delegated spending works.
</Note>
